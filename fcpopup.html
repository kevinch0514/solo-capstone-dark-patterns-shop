Forced Continuity

You've shopped a Dark Pattern.

It's called Forced Continuity.

Perhaps, you don't know what it was, but it's very widespread.

Essentially, Forced Continuity is a Dark Pattern where you sign up for a free trial.

Once your free trial ends, the service silently and automatically charges you from your credit card.

Yes, the service did inform you...in very tiny text or somewhere hard to see.

In any case, the automatic charges after a free trial often comes without any warning.

Furthermore, canceling the membership can be difficult.

Now you learned about Forced Continuity.
