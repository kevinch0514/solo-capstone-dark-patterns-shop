Roach Motel

You've shopped a Dark Pattern.

How hard was it to find the unsubscribe link?

Quite hard I'm guessing.

This is a Dark Pattern called Roach Motel.

Essentially, Roach Motel is a Dark Pattern where you find it easy to subscribe to a service, but difficult to unsubscribe.

It can be difficult to unsubscribe if the unsubscribe link is hidden among a bunch of text, at the very bottom of the page, and with no distinguishing feature such as a contrasting color or underline.

This isn't the only example of a Roach Motel.

Some Roach Motels makes it difficult to delete your account such that you must as a representative to help you (Amazon).

Roach Motel is a Dark Pattern that makes it hard for you as a consumer to unsubscribe so that services can make a profit and spam you.
